ADHD Task Hub

ADHD Task Hub is a local-first task management app for adults with ADHD who want calmer structure, clearer priorities, and a more practical way to manage everyday responsibilities.

  • Task management
  • Offline-first
  • Android-first
Built for

Adults who want a calmer, more supportive way to manage tasks, routines, and life admin

Approach

Local-first product design with practical structure, clear priorities, and low-friction task flows

What the app does

A quieter home base for the next step.

Capture and organize

Create lists, add tasks, notes, and subtasks, and keep your daily responsibilities organized without needing an account.

Surface what matters

Today, Hotlist, and Due Today views help important work stand out without turning the entire app into a stress signal.

Support follow-through

Use reminders, quiet hours, Focus Mode, and templates to move from intention to action with less setup overhead.

Core features

Designed for practical daily use.

Today and Hotlist views

See urgent tasks, due items, and high-priority work across your lists in one place.

List management

Create multiple lists, switch between full and compact views, and reorder tasks to match your workflow.

Templates

Start from reusable routines like Home Reset and Grocery Run, or build custom templates for repeatable tasks.

Task detail controls

Add notes, subtasks, due dates, reminders, recurrence, effort, and energy details in one place.

Optional task photos

Attach a photo to a task by taking one with your camera or choosing one from your photo library when you want visual context.

Local backup and restore

Export your data to a local backup file and restore it later without relying on a backend service.

Design direction

Calm foundations with room to evolve.

Calm Warm

A soft, low-clutter default designed to feel steady and welcoming.

Stitch

A more structured visual system with stronger hierarchy and clearer emphasis.

Aurora Glass

An atmospheric frosted-glass direction with a more expressive visual treatment.

Data model

Tasks, subtasks, lists, templates, focus sessions, reminders, app settings, and optional task photos stay on-device.

Privacy

Camera and photo-library access are optional and used only when you choose to attach a task photo.

What it does not require

No login, no team accounts, no backend, and no forced cloud sync for core use.

Release status

Currently preparing for internal testing on Google Play.

Good fit for

People who want a calmer task manager for home, admin, routines, and next-step planning without the heaviness of a large project tool.

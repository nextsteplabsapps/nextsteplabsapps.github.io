First release

ADHD Task Hub is built for the moment when everything feels equally loud.

The app keeps core task management local to the device, then adds practical views for today, urgent work, focus sessions, recurrence, and optional planning help.

01

Capture before it disappears

Quick Add, notes, reminders, and list choices help users get the thought out of their head while it is still fresh.

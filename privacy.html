Privacy Policy

Privacy for ADHD Task Hub, in plain language.

Last updated May 13, 2026. ADHD Task Hub is published by Next Step Labs and designed so core task management works on your device without a required account. This policy explains what data stays local, what optional features may use outside services, and how to contact us.

Short version

  • Core task data is local-first on your device.
  • No account is required for core use.
  • Photos, reminders, backups, AI, ads, and purchases are optional flows.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who provides the app

ADHD Task Hub and this website are provided by Next Step Labs. You can contact us at nextsteplabsapps@gmail.com.

2. Core app data stored on your device

Tasks, lists, notes, subtasks, checklist items, templates, focus sessions, reminders, app settings, theme preferences, and similar productivity data are stored locally on your device for core app use. ADHD Task Hub does not require a Next Step Labs account or automatic cloud sync for these core features.

3. Task photos, camera, and photo library

If you choose to add a task photo, the app may ask for camera permission so you can take a picture, or photo-library permission so you can select an image. These permissions are optional. When a photo is attached, the image is copied into the app's local storage so it can appear with the task.

4. Notifications and reminders

If you enable notifications, ADHD Task Hub can schedule local reminders on your device for tasks you choose. Reminder behavior depends on your Android settings, device permissions, and system scheduling rules.

5. Local backups, exports, and sharing

The app may let you export a local backup file and restore it later. Exporting, saving, or sharing a backup is controlled by the destination you choose on your device. If you import a backup, it may replace current local app data. Be careful with backup files because they can contain personal task information.

6. Optional AI Plan Assist

If you choose to use AI planning help, selected task or planning context needed for that request may be sent to a Next Step Labs backend and an AI service provider, such as OpenAI, to generate a response. AI Plan Assist is optional. Do not include information you do not want processed by those services, and review AI output before acting on it.

7. Purchases, Plus, and subscriptions

Optional Plus features may be purchased through Google Play. We may use RevenueCat to help manage purchase status and app entitlements. Next Step Labs does not receive your full payment card number from Google Play or RevenueCat.

8. Ads

Free versions or free experiences may display ads using Google Mobile Ads or related Google services. Ad providers may process device, app, advertising, and usage signals according to their own policies and your device settings. Plus may remove ads where offered.

9. Analytics, diagnostics, and reliability

Some builds may include limited analytics, crash, or reliability diagnostics to help us understand app health and improve the product. We aim to avoid collecting task titles, notes, task photos, and other user-written task content in analytics or diagnostics.

10. Website and support email

This static website may be served by GitHub Pages. If you email us, your message, email address, device details, screenshots, or other information you choose to include may be used to respond to you and keep a support record.

11. How data is shared

We do not sell your personal data. Core task data is intended to remain on your device unless you choose to export, share, restore, or use an optional service that requires data to be processed outside the device, such as AI Plan Assist, purchases, ads, analytics, diagnostics, or support.

12. Retention and deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, clear app storage, uninstall the app, or replace it by importing a backup. Support emails may be retained for as long as reasonably needed to respond, maintain support records, and improve the product.

13. Children

ADHD Task Hub is intended for personal productivity use by adults. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as ADHD Task Hub changes. When we make material changes, we will update the date above and post the current policy on this website.

Contact

Questions about privacy or app support?

Email Next Step Labs for privacy questions, support, closed-test feedback, or general contact related to ADHD Task Hub.

Helpful details

What to include in a support email.

Device

Phone and Android version

Include the device model, Android version, and whether you use gesture navigation or 3-button navigation.

App

App version and build

In ADHD Task Hub, Settings shows the app version and build. Add that to the message when you can.

Steps

What happened first

Tell us the screen, button, or flow you used before the issue, especially for cold-launch or first-tap bugs.

Media

Screenshots if useful

A screenshot or screen recording can help with layout, keyboard, bottom-sheet, or navigation-bar issues.
